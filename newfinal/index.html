About us

We are here to bringing a little extra dose of activism to the metaverse through storytelling, experiences, and community

GnarlyWorkz is the home for our professional and experimental projects. We are a startup blockchain technology company that develops NFTs and digital collectibles. We specialize in the fields of blockchain art, and digital media. GnarlyWorkz was founded in 2022 by a group of activists in Athens, Greece. The goal behind our projects is earning, in order to give back. We do our little bit of good; it is those little bits of good put together that can overwhelm the world
